If You Don’t Look Good, I Don’t Look Good.

Hi, I’m Ted an experienced hairdresser from London.

I guess my career began by cutting my family’s and friends hair in my bedroom at my parent’s house.

I always had a passion for hair styling and cutting since I was a little kid. I remember sitting in the kitchen watching my grandmother with scissors in her hand and bringing people joy and happiness by cutting and styling their hair which gave them outstanding change to their image.

I have received my hairdressing qualification at the age of 20, since then I have worked for an Award Winning Stonehills Salon and I’m currently working at The Hair Sanctuary.

I love what I do and I consider myself very fortunate to be in this industry.
